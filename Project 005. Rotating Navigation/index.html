Depressed

Seek professional help, such as therapy or counseling. Build a support system with friends and family. Establish a routine with regular exercise, healthy eating, and sufficient sleep. Challenge negative thoughts and focus on positive aspects. Engage in activities you enjoy. Remember, it's okay to ask for help.

Triumph

Rediscover joy by celebrating small victories. Cultivate gratitude for daily blessings. Engage in activities that bring fulfillment. Nurture positive relationships. Embrace new hobbies and adventures. Prioritize self-care and mental well-being. Revel in the beauty of the present moment. Celebrate the resilience that led to overcoming depression, savoring the newfound joy.
